Good Bard

Storytelling Atlas for Products

Good Bard helps you discover products through the stories they tell.

If you choose to care about some of the products you find, we'll take your hand and guide you through a journey of deep curated insights and answer the questions you didn't know to ask.

And if a product is not for you? We'll give you the information you need to decide if you should steer clear of it.

Imagine a magical place full a of wares, but that is not a store.

Picture a guide that helps you understand those wares deeply, but who is not a salesman.

Imagine a narrator that points you to the wares you should care about, but who is not a marketer.

We are creating that magical place.
